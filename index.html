About Us

Connecting Indonesia's natural harvest to international buyers, reliably.

Founded in Medan, North Sumatra, PT. Globalindo Sumber Lestari is a dedicated Indonesian export company specializing in candlenut — one of the region's most valued agricultural commodities. We bridge the gap between Indonesia's productive farms and buyers in global markets.

We partner closely with local farmers and suppliers to maintain a traceable, consistent supply chain. Every shipment is backed by careful quality control, proper export documentation, and a genuine commitment to sustainable, long-term trade.

Our Product

Indonesian Candlenut — premium quality, ready for export.

Candlenut (kemiri) is our flagship export commodity. Sourced from trusted North Sumatran farms, our candlenut is processed to meet international standards and shipped to buyers across Asia, the Middle East, and beyond.

🌿 Product: Candlenut (Kemiri)

Whole dried candlenut, export-grade. Available in bulk quantities to meet commercial and industrial buyer needs.

📦 Packaging

Packed in standard export sacks or customized packaging per buyer requirements, with proper labeling and weight compliance.

🚢 Shipping Origin

Shipped from Belawan Port, Medan — a major international trade port in North Sumatra with established freight connections.

How We Work

From Indonesia's fields to your destination.

A clear, reliable process — from farm sourcing through quality checks to export-ready delivery.

01

Farmer & Supplier Sourcing

We work directly with trusted local farmers and suppliers in North Sumatra to secure a consistent and traceable supply of candlenut.

02

Selection & Quality Control

Each batch is manually sorted and inspected to meet export-grade standards — ensuring size uniformity, moisture levels, and visual quality.

03

Export Packaging

Products are packed according to international trade requirements, clearly labeled, and prepared for safe long-distance shipping.

04

Documentation & Compliance

We prepare all required export documentation, including certificates of origin, phytosanitary certificates, and shipping documents.

05

Global Shipping & Delivery

Goods are shipped via trusted freight partners from Belawan Port, Medan — delivered reliably to buyer destinations worldwide.
